Your blood sugar is very low.

Try the 15-15 rule:

  • Eat or drink 15 grams of carbohydrates
  • Wait 15 minutes
  • Check blood sugar again. If it is still below 70 mg/dL, repeat

What contains 15 grams of carbohydrates?

  • 4 glucose tablets
  • 1/2 cup (4 ounces) of fruit juice
  • 5-6 hard candies
  • 1 tabelspoon (15 mL) of sufar, honey, or syrup

Avoid eating too much too fast to avoid a blood sugar spike.

If blood sugar level is in this range for more than 30 minutes, serious harm can occur, so we recommend calling a medical professional.

Symptoms of hypoglycemia (low blood sugar) that you may experience:

  • Weakness
  • Feeling tired
  • Shaking
  • Sweating
  • Headache
  • Hunger
  • Nervousness
  • Irritability
